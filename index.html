Commodities Market


A commodity market is where you can buy and sell goods taken from the earth, from cattle to gold,

oil to oranges, and orange juice to wheat. Commodities can be turned into products like baked

goods, gasoline, or high-end jewelry, which in turn are bought and sold by consumers and other

businesses. Markets in these goods are the oldest in the world, but they are as crucial to the

most modern societies as they were to the small trading communities of ancient civilizations.
